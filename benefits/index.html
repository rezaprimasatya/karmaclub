25% off Karma Group accommodation packages worldwide*

Receive 25% off any accommodation package at a Karma Group resort, even if it includes extras such as food and drink or spa treatments. *If you find an offer elsewhere that beats the best available rate on our website, we’ll provide 25% discount on the price you have found.

All bookings should be made through your Karma Club Concierge.

Amazing discounts at thousands of branded resorts globally

We’ve partnered with other travel companies worldwide to offer Karma Club members luxury accommodation at special discounted rates not available to the general public. Choose from a portfolio of resorts, hotels, lodges, cottages & other accommodation for a fraction of the price.

Ask your Karma Club Concierge for more details.

US$300 vouchers for Karma Group Accommodation

Within your Karma Club membership pack, we’ll also enclose 3 x US $100 vouchers to spend towards the cost of accommodation at any participating Karma Group resort. You can use one voucher per stay on bookings of 3 nights or more.

Remember to take a voucher with you to redeem it.

25% off Karma Spa, food & beverage plus more

Show your Karma Club Membership Card when ordering or paying for food, drink, spa treatments or other onsite guest services, and receive a 25% deduction. There are no maximum or minimum spend restrictions.

Automatic upgrade to best available rooms or villas

We’d like to offer you the best experience possible from the moment you check in. Show your membership card to the receptionist and you will automatically be upgraded to the best unit room or villa available – a potential value of hundreds of dollars per night.

Late checkout

We want your departure to be as pleasurable as your stay, so we extend the resort checkout time for Karma Club members by an extra 4 hours. Take the time to enjoy a last swim in the infinity pool, sip a glass of wine in the bar, or plan your next Karma visit.

Free exclusive gift pack

Our Karma receptionists will treat you to exclusive check-in services, including a free gift pack. The contents will be a surprise but think wellness, indulgence and fun. Karma Club Members are the only guests to receive this extra pack of goodies to help make their stay even more enjoyable.

Free kids club and babysitting

Most Karma Group resorts have a Three Monkeys Kids Club onsite and offer a babysitting service. As a Karma Club member, both the babysitting service and entry to the Three Monkeys Kids Club are absolutely free, so every member of the family can enjoy a well-deserved break.

Access to VIP parties, lifestyle and networking events

Karma understands the value of experiences, that’s why we offer Karma Club members exclusive invitations, both at our resorts and even close to your hometown. Past events include rugby weekends, gourmet retreats, cultural excursions, festive parties, wine-tasting and live music.

Complimentary Karma Beach membership

Stay at a Karma Resort in Bali, Fiji or the Bahamas and enjoy VIP access to our famous Karma Beach club. Thatched roofs and bamboo pavilions on the sand, sumptuous food, bespoke cocktails and the best sounds this side of Ibiza. Karma Club members can visit at no entry fee.

Free CDs and downloads of bespoke Karma playlists

Every Karma experience is sound-tracked with a bespoke playlist, curated exclusively for Karma Group by our in-house DJ’s and music aficionados. We make all our playlists and CDs available to Karma Club members for you to enjoy long after you have left the dance floor.

24-hour Karma Club Concierge service

We provide expert advice and a personalised service 24/7, wherever you are in the world. A Karma Club Concierge can help book your next holiday, give advice about travel options and excursions, or help with any non travel related lifestyle needs.

See our Contact Us page or your membership card for the toll-free number.

Monthly Newsletter with further offers

Our regular Karma Lifestyle newsletter will bring you the latest resort updates, news and events, including additional discounts and offers to give you the maximum benefit from your Karma Club membership.

Mobile updates via the Karma app
